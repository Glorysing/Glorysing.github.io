Featured Project: Doom 1.2 (Floppy Version)

Doom (1993) by id Software is one of the most influential first-person shooters in video game history. Version 1.2, distributed on floppy disks, represents an early stage of PC game distribution and shareware culture. Its preservation on the Internet Archive demonstrates how digital games transition from commercial products into cultural artifacts.
